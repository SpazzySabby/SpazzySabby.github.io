All About Moi

I am 38 years old and this is my first ever attempt at college, as I did not know my capacity for learning until recently when I went to adult high school and realized I was missing YouTube when I went to high school the first time. I have been in customer service for 21 years with my first job ever being at McDonald's, since I've always been rather willful I have done many, many different jobs from delivering pizza to retail to being a bartender on a cruise ship, (it's harder to fire you in the middle of the ocean!). I have been managing a busy brunch restaurant for the last five years and to be frank, I'm over it, I made my life too easy. Also, people have become too peopley. I would eventually like to work with youth addicts but I don't want to live off of peanuts doing it, enter IMD, a program I let the universe guide me towards, that I am actually super excited to be in. I did not know a program like this existed and to have a little bit of everything that's ever interested me all in one place is like hitting the learning jackpot.

My childhood was both glorious and traumatic, I grew up with the best of both worlds, half the time I spent with my grandparents (paternal) on their horse farm outside Ottawa, this is where my love for nature and animals grew, animals love unconditionally, humans do not. The other half I spent with my grandmother (maternal) in London, ON going to school. Around my 13 th birthday I stopped going to my grandparents in Ottawa and lived full time with my grandmother in London, and to this day there is still no good reason why. I was a really good student all therough elementary school, with my marks generally being in the 80's, once I hit high school however that all changed. I did not have the confidence to do well and ended up dropping out because I thought I was stupid, a narrative I know now to be false but one I held on to for many years. I spent my 20's having lots of fun, making lots of great memories, partying everyday! At 30 for some reason a switch was flipped and I went from functional partier to unfunctional alcoholic, I would say overnight, others would say years in the making. Either way, May 2016 I had my last drink and I have been sober ever since, it's the best journey I've been on yet, also the hardest. For the first time in my life I'm figuring out who I am and what I want, even making goals and 5 year plans! I am choosing to make the rest of my story filled with love, happiness and a commitment to helping others, it's just a fucking hard ass road getting there, but I will get there! My life quote is c'est la vie, it is tattooed on my foot and holds special meaning. My blockquote is just one of my favorite quotes in general, not something to live by, but obviously a part of me believes it or I wouldn't love it so much!

"If you're not first you're last"Ricky Bobby, Talledega Nights
